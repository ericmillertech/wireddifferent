operator profile

A Fellow ADHD Learner.
Still in the Trenches.

Eric Miller-Saisi — Founder, Miller Innovations LLC

I started my career in customer support — and I got good at it. Good enough to work for some of the top companies in the industry. But the whole time I was pushing toward something bigger: a transition into cybersecurity.

When I made that move, I hit a wall I wasn't expecting. Not the technical material — the way it was taught. Course after course, nothing designed for someone whose brain works the way mine does. No structure for ADHD learners. No honest conversation. Just "here's the material, keep up."

That gap is why I built Wired Different. A space where learning is actually engaging — real tools, real steps, real community. We're in the pilot phase right now and every response you give shapes what comes next.
