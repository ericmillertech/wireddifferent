About Eric

A Fellow ADHD Learner, Still in the Trenches

Eric Miller-Saisi — Founder, Miller Innovations LLC

I started my career in customer support — and I got good at it. Good enough to work for some of the top companies in the industry. I know what it means to show up, solve problems under pressure, and communicate clearly. But the whole time, I was building toward something bigger: a transition into cybersecurity.

When I made that move, I ran into a wall I wasn't expecting. Not the technical material — the way it was taught. Course after course, resource after resource, and nothing was designed for someone whose brain works the way mine does. No structure for ADHD learners. No honest conversation about the struggle. Just "here's the content, keep up."

That gap is why I built Wired Different. I wanted to create a space where learning is actually engaging — where there are real tools, real steps, and a real community for people navigating this path with ADHD. This platform is built on your feedback. We're in the pilot right now, and every response, every story, every survey answer shapes what comes next. Let's build this together.
