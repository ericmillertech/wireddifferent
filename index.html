// the why

Still in the trenches.
Just like you.

I'm not a researcher. I'm not a doctor. I came from customer support at some of the top companies in the industry — and decided I wanted to break into cybersecurity. With ADHD. Without a roadmap built for how my brain actually works.

I tried the bootcamps. I bought the courses. I joined the Discord servers. I did everything you're supposed to do — and I still spent almost a year working toward a cert that should have taken six months. Not because I wasn't capable. Because the structure assumed a brain I don't have.

There were career changes. Real moments of doubt. Times I almost walked away. But I kept going. And now that Security+ hangs on my wall — not as a finish line, but as a reminder that this is just the beginning.

The tools existed. The community existed. The mentorship didn't. The structure that accounts for hyperfocus, distraction cycles, and non-linear learning — that didn't exist either.

— Eric M., Founder · Miller Innovations LLC

Wired Different isn't a course platform. It's not a bootcamp. It's what I'm building in public, with the people who need it most, so no one has to spend a year on a six-month goal again. We're learning together — and your feedback is what gets built next.
